About YVJewels

Welcome to YVJewels, where the timeless art of Persian miniature painting meets the elegance of modern jewelry. My journey began in the heart of Iran, inspired by the intricate details of ancient manuscripts and the vibrant stories they tell.

Each piece I create is not just jewelry; it's a narrative told through the medium of gemstones and precious metals. Here, I blend traditional techniques with contemporary design to bring you:

  • Rings where every stroke of paint is a story.
  • Necklaces that carry the legacy of Persian culture.
  • Earrings and more, each piece a work of art.

The process of painting on gemstones is both meticulous and magical, transforming each stone into a canvas for miniature masterpieces. My commitment to quality ensures that every gemstone is ethically sourced, and every piece is crafted with the utmost care.

Join me in this journey through time and art. Each piece you wear is not just an accessory but a piece of history, reimagined.
